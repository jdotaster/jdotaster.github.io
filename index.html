About Me

I have been developing software professionally since 2021. I enjoy the mix of creative and logical thinking that is required to build software, and the continuous learning that accompanies it. In particular, I enjoy designing and building websites. I use a mobile-first approach in order to make my web designs as visually pleasing and accessible as possible. I believe that the internet should be an equitable space for everyone of all walks of life to learn and grow, and where everyone can benefit from the accessibility of information and tools.

I spend most of my spare time outdoors. When I'm not writing code, you'll usually find me with my adventure crew - my wife and two dogs. We love camping, hiking, fishing, rock climbing, and mountain biking, and I'm lucky enough to live in a place where all of those things can practically be done right in my back yard. I also enjoy cooking and gardening.
